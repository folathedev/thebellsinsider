BELLS TECH VICE VICE-CHANCELLOR CONSOLES WITH CRAWFORD VICE-CHANCELLOR

The Vice-chancellor, Bells University of Technology, Ota, Prof. Jeremiah Ojediran on Wednesday, 27th April, 2022 paid a condolence visit to the Vice-Chancellor Crawford University, Igbesa, Prof. Reuben Jiya Kolo on the untimely demise of his wife Mrs. Janet Oluwaremilekun Kolo.

Professor Ojediran commiserated with his Crawford counterpart on the huge loss and prayed that the good Lord grants the bereaved and his family the fortitude to bear this irreparable loss.

Prof. Kolo thanked and appreciated the Vice-chancellor and other members of Bellstech Management on the condolence visit for their concern and human empathy shown to him during this trying times. He assured them that he is finding strength in God while praying that such will also not be their portion.
