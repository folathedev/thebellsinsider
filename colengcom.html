VICE-CHANCELLOR COMMISSIONS NEW COLENG COLLEGE OFFICE

The Vice-Chancellor, Professor Jeremiah O. Ojediran, RE (COREN), MNSE, has commissioned the newly upgraded College Office of the College of Engineering (COLENG). The commissioning took place on Wednesday, 15th December 2021.

While welcoming the Vice-Chancellor and his Management Team, the Dean, expressed the intentional move of the College in aligning with the rebranding philosophy of the Professor Ojediran-led administration. According to him, beyond the facelifting of the office, the upgrade would also provide the needed ambience for effective administrative operations as well as research activities. The Dean also mentioned the supportive roles played by the Alumni as well as Heads of Departments in the College towards the successful completion of the project.

In his response, the VC expressed appreciation to the College for taking on this laudable project geared towards beautifying the College and by extension, the University. He commended the College for taking this giant stride, noting that Bellstech was poised towards becoming a model Ivory Tower with world-class and enviable edifices.

The VC and members of his team thereafter commissioned the Offices.
